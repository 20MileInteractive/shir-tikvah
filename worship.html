Worship at Shir Tikvah

Prayer at Shir Tikvah is a warm, participatory, and joyful experience, accessible no matter your background, how much or how little Hebrew you know, or whether you’re six or sixty-six. Music is a central component, an evocative blend of Hasidic, traditional, and contemporary melodies and words, intended to open our hearts and minds to prayer and contemplation. The spirit is contagious, and you will find yourself singing along, humming, or tapping your foot—or all three!

Whether in our beautiful sanctuary, outdoors, or in each other’s homes, prayer at Shir Tikvah builds our community from the inside out.
